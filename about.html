ABOUT US

How it started?.......

      We strive to bring the region’s culture to our cafes, coffees, and food. Landskap is the craftsmanship and livelihood of coffee farmers. It is a sustainable community, filled with health and meaning. Our cafés aspire to provide the ideal environment for a friendly gathering, a quick pick-me-up, or space to relax and recoup. Fika with friends, colleagues, or family to share a cup of coffee and some sweets.Our mantra, “Unculture Yourself”, a message we unravel with every new revolution and concept. We dare to help our customers encounter a new experience, every time they visit us.We began our journey in 2004 with the launch of our first flagship store in the diamond city, Chennai. Since then we are growing exponentially year by year and by the end of 2021, we plan to launch a total of 100 outlets across the country.
